About BG Remove Now

BG Remove Now is a free tool for removing backgrounds from photos, built for anyone who needs a quick transparent Remove Now — sellers prepping product photos, students working on projects, or anyone tidying up a picture.

Unlike most background remover tools, BG Remove Now does all of its processing directly on your device. Your image is never sent to a server, which means it's private, and it's fast because there's no upload or download of your original file.

The tool is free to use and supported by ads, which help keep it that way.
