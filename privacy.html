Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

How BG Remove Now handles your images

BG Remove Now removes image backgrounds entirely inside your own browser, using on-device processing. Your photos are never uploaded to, or stored on, any server. Once you close or reload the page, the image is gone.

Cookies and analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand visits and traffic (e.g. page views, general location, device type). This helps us improve the tool. Analytics does not have access to the images you process.

Advertising

This site does not currently display ads. If that changes in the future (for example, via Google AdSense), this policy will be updated to explain what data may be used and how to opt out.
